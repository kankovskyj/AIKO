ABOUT AIKO

Moscow born, Czech republic raised and London based, Aiko’s music is a unique blend of cultures and influences. In her music, she often explores subjects such as solitude, love and understanding of self.

Aiko started her career with an appearance in a TV show “Superstar”. From that point on she actively pursued her musical career and in August 2018 she released her debut EP, “AIKO”. Following up the successful release, she had many interviews, press mentions and radio airplay.

Performing at festivals such as Sziget, Waves Vienna, main stages of Praha Zije Hudbou, Good fest, or Sofar Sounds Budapest, Newcastle, opening for acts such as Black Honey, Tamino or Lauren Ruth Ward, Aiko is also an experienced performer.

She has also performed live for many session, such as BalconyTv Milan, Black Coffee session, Ocko obyvak session or DiscovrTV London. Aiko is now actively producing and releasing new music.
